Our Story & Vision

Discover our journey, values, and commitment to excellence in medical diagnostics since 2015.

Leading Diagnostic Excellence in Owerri

Founded in 2015, Westgard Medical Laboratory & Diagnostics has grown to become one of Owerri's most trusted diagnostic centers. What began as a small laboratory has evolved into a state-of-the-art facility equipped with the latest diagnostic technology and staffed by certified medical professionals.

Our journey has been guided by a simple yet powerful mission: to provide accurate, timely, and reliable diagnostic services that empower better healthcare decisions for both patients and medical practitioners.

Our Journey

Milestones in our journey towards diagnostic excellence

2015

Foundation

Westgard Diagnostics opens its doors with a small laboratory offering basic pathology services in Owerri.

2017

Expansion

Introduction of advanced hematology and chemistry analyzers, expanding our test menu to over 100 parameters.

2019

Imaging Services

Launch of our radiology department with digital X-ray and ultrasound services, becoming a comprehensive diagnostic center.

2021

Modernization

Complete laboratory renovation with state-of-the-art equipment and implementation of Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS).

2023

Advanced Testing

Introduction of molecular diagnostics and genetic testing services, including DNA testing and specialized fertility profiles.
